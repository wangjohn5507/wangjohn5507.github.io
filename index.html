👋Welcome

I am a new graduate student at National University of Kaohsiung (NUK) and currently applying for a M.S. program (Computer Science) in Canada. My research interests include but are not limited to Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Data Analytics. I was a member of the Computational Biology & Intelligence System Lab supervised by Prof. Tzu-Hsien Yang. My objective is to apply my knowledge to real-world problems and hopefully improve people’s life quality with technology.
